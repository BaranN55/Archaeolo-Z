About

You are an archaeologist who has been sent to investigate About gen-Z. As you explore under the surface of
the earth, you discover that it is filled with dangerous traps and puzzles that you must solve in order to
uncover the secrets of gen-Z. Your goal is to find the hidden treasures and and sell them to gain the
Currency Which is called Roasted Goldfish.

On the side of the page you always have three buttons. The first one is The coin button, which shows you how
much Roasted Goldfish you have. The second one is the Collected Artifacts button, which shows you all the
artifacts you have collected so far and how much they cost, and also if you already sold them or not.

The third one is the Tools button, which shows you all the tools you have collected so far and how much
they cost, and also the new tools that you can purchase; because you can't go to the next level if you
don't have the better tool.

You can use spacebar to dig the ground and collect artifacts. And after some levels you have to answer
the questions to could pass to the next level.
